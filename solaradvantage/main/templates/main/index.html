What We Provide

SolarAdvantage pulls together in-depth information about the advantages and cost of solar energy in your area. We aim to provide easy to understand analytics to help you understand the potential gain investing in solar power and if it's the right choice for you. Decisions are based on weather data in the area over the past decade and an estimation of kWh per day of potential energy generation. We then compare this to energy costs in the area.

Why is Solar Energy so Important?

No greenhouse gas emissions are released into the atmosphere when you use solar panels to create electricity. And because the sun provides more energy than we'll ever need, electricity from solar power is a very important energy source in the move to clean energy production.

  • Gives you control over your electricity
  • Provides clean, renewable energy
  • Increases home value
  • Qualifies for tax breaks and cash incentives
  • Costs have fallen
